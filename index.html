Tournament Overview

Experience the thrill of competitive volleyball in the heart of the SF Bay Area

Welcome to California Cup - Fall 2025

The California Cup is the recreational volleyball event in Northern California, bringing together the best teams from across the region for one epic day of intense competition, camaraderie, and sportsmanship.

Join us on Saturday, October 25th, 2025 from 1:00 PM to 7:00 PM at Camden Community Center in San Jose for an incredible day of volleyball action that celebrates the spirit of the game.

Tournament History

Celebrating over a decade of volleyball fun and excitement

2010

Inception

The Bay Area Volleyball Tournament was founded by local volleyball enthusiasts with a vision to create a premier competitive event in Northern California.

2015

Expansion

Tournament expanded to include multiple divisions and attracted teams from across the West Coast, establishing itself as a regional powerhouse.

2020

Innovation

Introduced live streaming and digital scoreboards, making the tournament accessible to fans worldwide during challenging times.

2025

Future

This year marks our 15th anniversary with enhanced facilities, expanded prize pools, and the introduction of youth divisions.
